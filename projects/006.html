MARTA Mapp transit Experiment

Background

A self initiated experimental project. Where I intended on getting a better understanding of how maps are designed. This project by no means polished.

The current MARTA Rail map consists of a traditional layout that mimics an abstract form of Atlanta, Georgia.

The problem was reading the map required viewing the station names in a sideways manner.

Enter my experiment the goal was to attempt to re-design the map with an isometric view.
